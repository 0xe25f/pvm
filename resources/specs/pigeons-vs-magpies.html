Pigeons vs Magpies

An urban park turf war. You command the Pigeons. Gather breadcrumbs & twigs, train a flock, and raze every Magpie building.

WASD / Arrows / edge — pan camera
Left-click — select  ·  drag — box select  ·  Shift — add
Right-click — move / attack / gather
Bottom buttons — train units, place buildings
Esc — cancel selection / placement

Destroy all enemy nests to win. Lose all of yours and it's over.
